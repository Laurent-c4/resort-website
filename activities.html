Resort Activities

At the InterContinental Tahiti Resort & Spa, you can enjoy a great array of activities, from sports, such as tennis and volleyball, to water-based activities and cultural pursuits. We also have a jogging track that winds through our tropical gardens, and a fitness centre so you can keep up with your workout programme while on vacation. We also stage a number of cultural demonstrations throughout the week, including:

  • Coconut show
  • Paero-dyeing
  • Basket weaving
  • Torch lighting at sunset
  • Handicrafts exposition
